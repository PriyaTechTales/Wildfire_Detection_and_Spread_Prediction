About Wildfire Watch

AI-powered detection and prediction to combat wildfires and save lives.

Our Mission

At Wildfire Watch, our goal is to leverage artificial intelligence to predict and detect wildfires in real time. By analyzing environmental data, we help authorities take swift action, mitigating damage and saving lives.

How It Works

Our deep learning models process satellite imagery, weather conditions, and geographical data to provide accurate wildfire forecasts. Real-time alerts ensure that response teams are always a step ahead.
